About

In a world where the importance of mental health is increasingly recognized, Wissenaire, the annual techno-management festival of IIT Bhubaneswar, is set to make a powerful statement by organizing a unique running race in collaboration with Pranya and Manam Foundations. The event, aptly titled "Run for Mental Health," aims to raise awareness about the significant impact of running on mental well-being.
Participants in the "Run for Mental Health" will not only contribute to a noble cause but also experience the joy of physical activity and the emotional rewards that come with it.
